Symptoms of
coronavirus

In the vast majority of cases, these symptoms are not
associated with the coronavirus, but with the usual SARS.

The main symptoms of coronavirus

high temperature

cough

impaired or lost sense of smell

loss of taste

muscle pain

Rare symptoms of coronavirus

headache

chest congestion

hemoptysis

diarrhea

nausea

Symptoms may occur within 14 days after contact with an infectious patient,
the symptoms are similar in many ways to many respiratory diseases,
often mimic the common cold, can resemble the flu.
