What to do?

Follow the rules of hygiene

To protect yourself from infection, first of all, you should
follow the rules of personal hygiene.

  • Keep your hands clean-wash them frequently with soap and water for at least 20 seconds,
    then wipe them dry with a disposable paper towel. It is highly advisable to carry antiseptic wipes or liquid products
    (gels, sprays, etc.). So you can always keep your hands clean, even if you can't wash them.
  • Try not to touch your mouth, nose, or eyes with unwashed hands (usually, we do this unconsciously on
    average 15 times an hour).
  • Use the mask when visiting public places and if you use public transport. Remember,
    the mask must be changed every two to three hours!
  • Regularly clean and disinfect surfaces and devices that you touch (computer or
    laptop keyboard, smartphone screen, remote controls, switches, and door handles).
    To fight viruses, it is best to use wipes and alcohol-based gels.
  • Carry disposable wipes with you and always cover your nose and mouth when you cough or sneeze. After use,
    they should be disposed of immediately.
  • Do not eat food (nuts, chips, cookies, and other snacks) from shared packages or dishes if other
    people have dipped their fingers
    in them.
  • Often ventilate the premises, regularly do a wet cleaning.
  • Greet each other without touching. Give preference to a gesture or wave your hand instead of a handshake.
  • Process the products you bring from the store. Vegetables and fruits should be washed, and goods in packages
    should be washed with soap and water, or treated with antibacterial wipes.
